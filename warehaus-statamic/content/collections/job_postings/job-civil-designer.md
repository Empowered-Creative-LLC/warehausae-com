overview: >-
  This position focuses on preparing site design and land development plans using Civil 3D, working closely with project managers and engineers. You’ll be involved in grading, stormwater, utilities,
  and permitting, with the opportunity to take ownership of portions of the design as you grow.
responsibilities:
  - Develop site design plans including grading, utilities, stormwater, and overall layout
  - Prepare subdivision and land development plans, including E&S, PCSM, NPDES, and HOP documentation
  - Interpret surveys, zoning ordinances, and engineering drawings to inform design decisions
  - Design site elements such as access and circulation, parking layouts, and utility systems
  - Coordinate with project managers, engineers, and outside partners to keep projects moving
  - Maintain organized, accurate plan sets and design files throughout each phase
  - Support project closeout with record drawings and final documentation
  - Manage portions of design work within project schedules and budgets
qualifications:
  - 3+ years of experience preparing land development plans
  - Proficiency in AutoCAD Civil 3D (grading, surfaces, alignments, plan production)
  - Working knowledge of stormwater management, E&S requirements, and permitting processes
  - Ability to interpret surveys, plans, and municipal regulations
  - Experience coordinating with municipalities or regulatory agencies
  - Strong attention to detail and communication skills
  - Ability to work within a collaborative, multidisciplinary team
  - Experience level will determine placement as Civil Designer or Senior Civil Designer
