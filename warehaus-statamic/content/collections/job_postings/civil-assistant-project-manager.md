overview: >+
  This position blends civil site design with project coordination. You’ll support Civil Project Managers with scheduling, communication, and permitting while continuing to produce plans in Civil 3D.
  It’s a strong fit for someone growing into project management but not ready to step away from design.

responsibilities:
  - Support Civil Project Managers in the coordination and delivery of land development projects
  - Manage portions of project production, including scheduling, task coordination, and team communication
  - Assist with permit applications and regulatory submissions
  - Participate in meetings with clients, municipalities, and project stakeholders
  - Maintain organized project documentation, files, and communication records
  - Contribute to QA/QC efforts to ensure plans meet Warehaus standards
  - Assist with scopes, budgets, schedules, and subconsultant coordination
  - Help guide junior staff and support team workflow as needed
  - Contribute to site design and plan preparation, including layout, grading, utilities, and stormwater
  - Prepare subdivision and land development plans, including E&S, PCSM, NPDES, and HOP documentation
  - Support project closeout with record drawings and final documentation
qualifications:
  - 5+ years of experience in land development design
  - Proficiency in AutoCAD Civil 3D (grading, surfaces, plan production)
  - Strong understanding of the land development process, from planning through permitting
  - Experience coordinating with municipalities, regulatory agencies, or review bodies
  - Exposure to project coordination, scheduling, or client communication
  - Strong organizational skills and attention to detail
  - Ability to balance technical work with project coordination responsibilities
  - Bachelor’s degree in Civil Engineering or related field
  - EIT certification preferred; candidates pursuing licensure encouraged
