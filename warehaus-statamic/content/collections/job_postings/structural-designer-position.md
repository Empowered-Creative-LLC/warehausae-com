  This position focuses on structural modeling, drafting, and design support across a range of projects. You’ll work alongside project managers and engineers to develop construction documents,

responsibilities:
  - Develop structural models and construction documents from schematic design through construction documentation
  - Perform structural detailing and support design development
  - Collect and verify field information to inform accurate design
  - Assist with shop drawing and submittal reviews, and respond to RFIs
  - Support QA/QC efforts to ensure accuracy and consistency in project deliverables
  - Coordinate with architecture, MEP, and civil teams to align design across disciplines
  - Participate in clash detection and coordination as required by project scope
  - Maintain organized design files and documentation throughout each phase
  - Follow and contribute to Warehaus standards for drawing production and file management
  - Coordinate with external consultants as needed
  - Assist with project closeout documentation
qualifications:
  - Bachelor’s degree in Structural Engineering or related field
  - 3+ years of structural design or production experience preferred
  - Proficiency in Revit
  - Working knowledge of structural analysis/design tools such as RISA, RAM, or Enercalc a plus
  - EIT certification or actively pursuing licensure preferred
  - Strong attention to detail and organization
  - Ability to work collaboratively within a multidisciplinary team
