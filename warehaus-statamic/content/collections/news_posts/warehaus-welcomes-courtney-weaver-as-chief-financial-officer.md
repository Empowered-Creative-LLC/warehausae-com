  **York, PA – March 9, 2026**


  **Warehaus,** a collaborative architecture and engineering firm based in York, Pennsylvania, has hired Courtney Weaver as Chief Financial Officer. Weaver joined the firm on March 2, 2026.


  As CFO, Weaver will lead Warehaus’ financial strategy and oversee all corporate accounting and reporting functions. She will guide the firm’s budgeting and forecasting processes, strengthen
  financial reporting systems, and support leadership and project teams with financial insight that helps drive informed decision-making across the organization.


  Weaver brings more than a decade of financial leadership experience, including several years supporting regional architecture, engineering, and construction firms. Throughout her career she has
  focused on improving financial transparency, modernizing reporting processes, and helping operational teams translate financial data into better business decisions.


  Her work has included implementing financial forecasting tools, streamlining reporting systems, and building operational dashboards that provide leadership teams with clearer visibility into project
  performance and company financial health.


  Weaver holds a Bachelor of Science in Accounting from Pennsylvania State University.


  “Courtney brings a thoughtful, forward-looking approach to financial leadership,” said David Koratich, co-owner and COO of Warehaus. “Her experience working alongside project teams and operations
  leaders will help strengthen our financial systems and support the continued growth of Warehaus.”
