intro_prose: >-
  WellSpan Health is set to enhance healthcare accessibility in the Shrewsbury area through the establishment of a community hospital. The focus of the project is the development of approximately 4.85
  acres along Wolfe Road in Hopewell Township, involving two vacant parcels. Rigorous planning and adherence to regulatory requirements are paramount to guarantee a comprehensive and successful
  development process.
narrative:
  we_listen: >-
    Warehaus has played a pivotal role in advancing the project through a spectrum of services. Initial tasks encompassed a Boundary and Topographic Survey, along with identifying existing utility
    infrastructure in the designated area. A crucial step in the process was New Utility Coordination, involving the acquisition of 'Will Serve' letters from local sewer and water service providers.
    The project also entailed the preparation and submission of Sewage Planning/Exemption to the Pennsylvania Department of Environmental Protection (PA DEP).
  we_design: >-
    Geotechnical Engineering was employed for structural and stormwater management testing, and a Sketch Plan was formulated to establish the initial site design and lot configuration. Subsequently,
    the project evolved to include the preparation of an NPDES (National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System) Individual Permit Application and Preliminary/ Final Land Development Plan.
    Additionally, anticipating the need for a Trip Generation Letter, Warehaus conducted a Preliminary Study of specific intersections, engaging a Traffic Subconsultant for this aspect. The
    comprehensive suite of services extended to cover Construction Administration Services.
  we_deliver: >-
    This project underscores Warehaus’ proficiency in navigating diverse facets of civil design and permitting for a significant healthcare facility. Their efforts ensure strict compliance with
    regulations, ultimately contributing to the development of vital infrastructure within the community.
