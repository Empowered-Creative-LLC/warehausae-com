intro_prose: >-
  Cove Forge Behavioral Health is undergoing an expansion in Williamsburg, PA, focused on enhancing its residential rehabilitation facilities to accommodate more individuals seeking addiction
  treatment. Warehaus, tasked with the architectural design, created three new two-story dormitories, each serving distinct functions. One dormitory includes a first-floor dining hall to support
  communal living of the entire campus and foster a sense of community among residents.
narrative:
  we_listen: >-
    The buildings were designed for efficiency and comfort, ensuring ease of access and operational flow for staff while promoting a therapeutic environment for residents. The layout emphasizes open,
    communal spaces alongside private areas for reflection and recovery. Warehaus integrated features to support Cove Forge Behavioral Health and their holistic approach to rehabilitation, ensuring
    that the design aligns with the center’s mission of long-term recovery.
  we_design: >-
    The dormitories are situated on pad-ready sites, and the buildings’ designs include elements that reflect Cove Forge’s values of integrity, teamwork, and clinical excellence. The architectural
    focus was on functionality and aesthetics, with attention to creating spaces that facilitate recovery for not only the new occupants but for others housed elsewhere on the site. Warehaus’ design
    incorporates natural light while maintaining privacy, soothing color schemes, and strategic room layouts to optimize treatment settings while enhancing the overall therapeutic atmosphere of the
    facility. The project is currently under construction.
  we_deliver: >-
    By expanding the treatment center’s capacity with thoughtful, recovery-focused architecture, Warehaus is helping to create an environment that fosters healing and supports both the staff and
    residents in their daily interactions and long-term goals. The project reflects a deep understanding of the specific needs of rehabilitation centers and embodies Cove Forge Behavioral Health and
    their mission to be a leader in addiction treatment.
