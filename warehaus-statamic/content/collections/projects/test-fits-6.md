intro_prose: >
  Our test fit services provide a preliminary floor plan to assess if a space can meet your specific needs. This quick and cost-effective process helps confirm room capacities, layout efficiencies,
  and operational requirements, guiding you toward well thought-out space plans. By ensuring all components fit cohesively and maintaining essential adjacencies, our test fits serve as a solid
  foundation for the schematic design phase, allowing you to make informed decisions with confidence.


  Modification an existing office space to accommodate a reduced footprint and a flexible remote work environment. The team designed workspaces for 16 employees, adjusting meeting space sizes, and
  creating supporting spaces for a hybrid in-person and remote setup, in an effort to determine whether the client could remain at the current location with these modifications or if they needed to
  seek a new office space.


  Investigation and selection of a new workspace for PayPal flagship office. Performed workspace analysis and developed test fit-outs to narrow the selection process. Different work type spaces –
  open, semi-private and private booths – were provided to acknowledge diversity in workstyles.


  Design and calculation of the layout for a potential office space in an existing building under new ownership. The design includes a reception area, three individual office spaces, a bullpen area
  for three people, and additional cubicle space if feasible.


  Due Diligence Study for Wellspan acquisitions – Test Fit of 2 floors, structural planning for removal of existing partial mezzanines and construction of new mezzanines for the entire floor plate,
  utility and site master planning. Close care was taken to presenting a vision for the exterior modification to provide a new façade to the building to purposefully transform the building use and
  function from its original intent to ensure ease of patient flow, function, parking and wayfinding.


  Due Diligence Study for Wellspan acquisitions – Test Fit of 2 floors, structural confirmations of loads to be applied to the existing floor systems, utility modifications master planning. Special
  intention was given to maintaining as much, if not all, of the historic structure and integrity. This study involved placement of two practices into the space, Family Medicine and a Dental Practice.


  Test Fit designs for the Wyomissing B and E buildings, including areas for nephrology and sleep apnea treatment. The spaces included restrooms, exam rooms, offices, storage, laundry, waiting room,
  kitchen, front desk and tech space.


  Test Fit designs for a first-floor restaurant in a high-profile, community-focused location.
