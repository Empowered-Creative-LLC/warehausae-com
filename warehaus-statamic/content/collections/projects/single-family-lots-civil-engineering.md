intro_prose: >-
  Warehaus completed the site planning and development for Lots 2, 3, and 4 along Church Road in Manchester Township, York County, Pennsylvania. The scope of services includes several key tasks. A
  comprehensive survey was conducted, resulting in an Existing Conditions Plan. Geotechnical investigation determined soil suitability for stormwater infiltration, influencing the Stormwater
  Management Plan required by township ordinance. An Erosion and Sediment Control Plan was prepared and submitted to the York County Conservation District. The Stormwater Management incorporated the
  site layout, grading, and erosion control details. Additionally, construction stakeout was coordinated, and a post-construction As-built Plan was prepared to satisfy municipal and county records. A
  Driveway Highway Occupancy Permit (HOP) was secured, and a Site Development Plan was created to support the building permit application, ensuring compliance with zoning and impervious cover limits.


  Prepared Stormwater Management and Erosion and Sediment Control Plans for single family home in Spring Garden Township.
