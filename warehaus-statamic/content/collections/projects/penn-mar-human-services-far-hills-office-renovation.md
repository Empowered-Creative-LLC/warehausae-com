lead_heading: Centralizing services into a unified hub
intro_prose: >-
  Penn-Mar Human Services embarked on a transformative renovation project at its Far Hills facility, situated in New Freedom, Pennsylvania. The project aimed to centralize administrative, operational,
  and programming services, consolidating dispersed functions into a unified hub. By optimizing space and streamlining operations, Penn-Mar sought to enhance its support programs and create a
  conducive environment for both team members and program participants. The new, open-office atmosphere connects Penn-Mar’s consumers and office staff, bringing them together for enhanced
  communication and support.
narrative:
  we_listen: >-
    Understanding the unique needs and challenges of Penn-Mar’s operations was paramount in the renovation process. Years of strategic planning and iterative space planning informed the project’s
    direction. Warehaus worked alongside the Penn-Mar team to understand the evolving demands of their services, adapting the facility to align with the community-centered model of support. The
    decision to consolidate operations at the Far Hills facility arose from a thorough analysis of space requirements and operational efficiencies.
  we_design: >-
    The design phase of the project focused on maximizing the functionality and flexibility of the Far Hills facility. Building upon Phase I renovations, which included specialized areas for training
    and therapy sessions, Phase II aimed to right-size operations and accommodate Pennsylvania-based programs. Customized settings conducive to concentrated learning were integrated into the design,
    reflecting Penn-Mar’s commitment to providing tailored support services.
  we_deliver: >-
    The Far Hills renovation project was successfully delivered on time and within budget. The completion of Phase II marks the culmination of years of planning and collaboration. With administrative,
    operational, and programmatic teams now centralized at the Far Hills facility, Penn-Mar is poised to achieve greater operational efficiencies and enhance the quality of its support programs. The
    renovated space not only meets the organization’s current needs but also positions Penn-Mar for continued success in fulfilling its mission of empowering individuals with intellectual and
    developmental disabilities.
