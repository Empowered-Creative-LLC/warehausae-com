narrative:
  we_listen: Warehaus was brought on board to make revisions and corrections to work done by another architectural design team that could not get approved by the local authority having jurisdiction.
  we_design: >-
    The team, with IB Abel as Electrical designer an IES as the Mechanical/Plumbing designer, began by reviewing existing design documents to assess areas of concern, with the intent to develop
    permittable architectural and MEP documents and bring the office design to code.
  we_deliver: >-
    The enhanced office now features a new reception space, followed by a corridor of six exam rooms. The rear exit was reconfigured to add an X Ray room, which features lead-lined drywall over
    existing drywall to meet physicist requirements. Warehaus submitted permits and provided state-certification information per X Ray technology requirements.
