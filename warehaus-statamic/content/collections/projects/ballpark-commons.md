intro_prose: >-
  Ballpark Commons in downtown York, PA, is a key initiative to enhance the area around WellSpan Park, home of the York Revolution baseball team. Led by the York County Industrial Development
  Authority (YCIDA), this 20,000 sf mixed-use development will feature hospitality and office spaces, revitalizing the North George Street entrance. The project addresses the city’s need for economic
  growth and social spaces.
narrative:
  we_listen: >-
    The first floor of the building will house a restaurant, establishing a vibrant destination for pre- and post-game activities. The second floor will be dedicated to office spaces, attracting local
    businesses and fostering year-round foot traffic. The project also integrates with Brooks Robinson Plaza leading to the ballpark.
  we_design: >-
    Funded in part by a $4 million RACP grant, Ballpark Commons is expected to generate $9 million in total investment, contributing significantly to downtown York’s development. This investment
    supports upgrades to WellSpan Park, such as a new videoboard and playing surface, further elevating the venue’s appeal.
  we_deliver: >-
    Construction is scheduled to begin in 2025, with the project positioned to become a model for future downtown redevelopment efforts. Situated within a historical review zone, the design will
    balance modern needs with architectural preservation, ensuring the new space aligns with the community’s heritage.
