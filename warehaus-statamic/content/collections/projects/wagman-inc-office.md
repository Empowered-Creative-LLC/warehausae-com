lead_heading: A Workspace Designed for Wagman’s Future
intro_prose: >-
  Warehaus partnered with Wagman to transform their new headquarters at 3265 Farmtrail Road in York, PA, into a dynamic workplace for Wagman, Inc., Wagman Heavy Civil, and Wagman Construction. The
  renovation focused on enhancing functionality, flexibility, and cost-efficiency while creating an environment that reflects Wagman’s culture and expertise. Large open workspaces encourage
  collaboration and adaptability, while flexible workstation layouts and hoteling spaces support a post-COVID hybrid work model. Amenities such as a centrally located break room with views of the
  surrounding green space, a gym/wellness area, and informal meeting spaces were incorporated to promote employee well-being and workflow efficiency.
narrative:
  we_listen: >-
    Throughout the building, the design balances raw industrial elements with refined finishes to create a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Exposed steel trusses, ductwork, and structural components
    highlight Wagman’s rugged craftsmanship, while natural wood accents and ambient lighting soften these elements to create comfort and elegance. Mechanical systems are deliberately left visible to
    celebrate the building’s infrastructure, with carefully organized ducts and plumbing lines integrated into the overall aesthetic. Even the sprinkler system serves as a visual accent, painted to
    contrast with the building’s clean white surfaces.
  we_design: >-
    The visitor experience begins at a bold new steel-framed entrance canopy., paired with a gabion seat wall that nods to Wagman’s dual expertise in construction and civil engineering. Inside,
    sliding glass fronts on private offices and meeting rooms create a sense of openness, while expansive perimeter glazing floods the space with natural light and connects the interior to its
    surroundings. A central circulation spine anchors the interior, guiding movement with skylights and pendant lighting while showcasing Wagman’s history along a gallery wall that tells pieces of the
    company’s story.
  we_deliver: >-
    The completed headquarters goes beyond serving as a functional office space—it embodies Wagman’s innovation and integrity. By harmonizing form and function, Warehaus delivered a space that
    inspires employees, welcomes clients, and engages visitors through its honest expression of industrial elegance. This new home positions Wagman for future growth while standing as a testament to
    the company’s legacy and forward-thinking vision.
