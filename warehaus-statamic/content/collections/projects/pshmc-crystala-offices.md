intro_prose: >-
  Warehaus conducted a corporate office masterplan to determine which “non-clinical” teams could be relocated to make room for more clinical program support staff. The study included examination of
  capacities of the 100, 200 and 75 Crystal A Buildings and multiple offsite leased building spaces. The overall master plan resulted in two projects – move management for 100 Crystal A, which was
  recently renovated, and full renovation of 200 Crystal A.
narrative:
  we_listen: >-
    After Phase I of the staff shift to 100 Crystal A was completed in 2018, Warehaus assisted the client with a full range of test fit options for the renovation and infrastructure upgrades required
    to occupy the 200 Crystal A Building. After construction documentation was complete, COVID completely changed the way the client was going to work in the future.
  we_design: >-
    Programming of the building was revisited, in depth, to define yet another new intent of the building and the groups that were to occupy it. The 200 Crystal A Building design and infrastructure
    upgrades were redesigned and the project is now complete.
  we_deliver: >-
    This renovation includes office design, data center design, complete data wiring and infrastructure upgrades, including tie into to campus wide support systems such as security, fire alarm,
    wireless, data and communication system and emergency power. A completely new interior finish palette was created integrating new branding standards and furniture.
