narrative:
  we_listen: >-
    Warehaus worked with Hampden TA, LLC to develop designs for The Flats at Hampden, two connected and one separate apartment buildings and one separate retail/ office building. In total, 120
    apartments are planned.
  we_design: >-
    The first building will have five stories and 62,676 sf of space and 15 one-bedroom apartments, 20 two-bedroom apartments and four three-bedroom apartments. It is connected to a four-story
    building with 68,050 sf of space and 15 one-bedroom apartments and 33 two-bedroom apartments. The connected building will also have a community space, conference/work space, fitness center and
    bike storage. The second stand-alone apartment building will have three stories and 45,783 sf of space and nine one-bedroom apartments and 24 two-bedroom apartments. Both apartment complexes will
    have parking on the lowest level.
  we_deliver: A third building, with 4,500 sf of commercial space, is also planned, with a tenant to be named at a later date. Construction begins in Summer 2021.
