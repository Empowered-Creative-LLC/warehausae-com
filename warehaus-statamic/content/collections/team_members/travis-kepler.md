bio: >-
  Travis Kepler has been practicing architecture for over 25 years, bringing deep experience across education, museums, retail environments, assisted living, religious facilities, and medical
  practices. His work spans a range of construction types and delivery methods, with a consistent focus on projects that demand thoughtful coordination of building systems.


  In his role as Architectural Process Director, Travis leads the design and documentation standards that shape every architectural project at Warehaus. He guides efforts to refine how drawings are
  developed, improve consistency across teams, and strengthen the integration of materials, codes, and construction methods. His work helps ensure that projects move forward with clarity,
  coordination, and a high level of quality.


  Travis is particularly experienced in alternative learning environments, where performance requirements for systems such as heating, cooling, humidity, and lighting are critical. He also brings a
  strong background in adaptive reuse, with a focus on integrating modern systems into existing and historically sensitive structures.


  His approach combines big-picture thinking with attention to detail, helping teams deliver work that is both well-designed and well-executed.
