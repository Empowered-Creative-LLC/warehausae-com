  ## Deep Roots. Rich History.


  Warehaus is an award-winning team of designers with a 45-year legacy serving Central Pennsylvania and beyond. What began as a land surveying company has evolved to become a 40-person firm offering
  [architecture](http://warehausae.com/services/architecture/), [civil engineering and land planning](http://warehausae.com/services/civil_engineering/), [structural
  engineering](http://warehausae.com/services/structural/), [interior design](http://warehausae.com/services/interior_design/), [historic preservation](http://warehausae.com/work/historic/), and
  [building sciences](http://warehausae.com/work/building-sciences/) services.


  Warehaus co-owners [Troy Bankert](http://warehausae.com/team/troy-bankert/) and [David Koratich](http://warehausae.com/team/david-koratich/) each have a long history at Warehaus, spanning as far
  back as 1988. Today, they oversee the day-to-day operations of the firm as well as overall asset management, guiding the strategic vision for ongoing growth and advancement.


  ![Troy Bankert and David Koratich, Warehaus Owners](/assets/imported/2025/09/Troy-Bankert-and-David-Koratich-2025-1-1024x683.jpg)


  ## Our History


  ### 1980


  * * *


  The Kinsley family of companies acquires Land Survey Consultants.


  ### 1980s-90s


  * * *


  The company, under various sub-entities, provides land survey consulting, civil and environmental services, architectural, interior and structural design to projects throughout the Mid-Atlantic
  region.


  ### 1998


  * * *


  All disciplines combine under the name LSC Design, Inc.


  ### 2017


  * * *


  ![Warehaus Logo](/assets/imported/2023/10/Warehaus_Office_Painted_Signage-06_20-0011.jpg)


  The company rebrands as Warehaus AE, inspired by a combination of the firm’s industrial roots and company culture.


  ### 2021


  * * *


  ![Troy Bankert, CEO and David Koratich, COO, Warehaus Co-Owners](/assets/imported/2023/03/Dave_Troy-03_21.jpg)


  Troy Bankert, AIA and David Koratich, EIT, two long-term employees, acquire independent ownership of Warehaus.


  ### 2022


  * * *


  ![Warehaus Office - Architects, Engineers and Designers](/assets/imported/2023/06/Warehaus-Office-Aerial-e1725551198583.webp)


  18 months after independent ownership, Warehaus purchases its office on North George Street in downtown York.
