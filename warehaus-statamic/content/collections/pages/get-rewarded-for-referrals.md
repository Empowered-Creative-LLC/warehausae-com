  ## Referrals can be rewarding!


  Know someone who would be a great fit for Warehaus’ Civil Designer role? You could receive $500\* for referring a contact to our open [Civil Designer
  role](http://warehausae.com/job/job-civil-designer/).


  \* The Fine Print:


  _Your referred contact must apply for the Civil Designer role online. Referrals must be submitted prior to candidate application to be eligible._


  _If your referred contact is hired for the Civil Designer role within 90 days of your referral submission, you will receive a $500 referral bonus, payable by check, provided the employee remains
  employed with Warehaus for at least 90 days._


  _Employees of Warehaus are not eligible for this reward._


  _To be eligible, all outlined steps must be followed. Payment will be issued after the referred employee completes 90 days of continuous employment._


  _Warehaus reserves the right to modify or discontinue this program at any time._
